Vy Group (formerly Norges Statsbaner) is the state railway company in [Norway](/country/norway). Together with its subsidiaries, it operates most of the passenger rail transport in Norway.
In addition to Vy, SJ Nord and Go-Ahead Nordic also operate services in Norway.

{{% highlight important %}}
In Norway, railway services are awarded in several lots by the Ministry of Transport and Communications through tendering procedures.
Unlike for most other FIP members, FIP Discounts are recognized not only by Vy, but also by SJ Nord and Go-Ahead Nordic. The FIP Coupon “Vy Group” is also valid on these railway companies.[^2]
{{% /highlight %}}

The [Vy network map](https://www.vy.no/files/eyx1eny7/vyno-production/1e6de6b67ae9aa2f456b4845da049a9998ea0047.pdf) and the [Oslo network map](https://www.vy.no/files/eyx1eny7/vyno-production/9dade0562e68cb1ec14644cdb1f443be1b29115c.pdf) can be viewed online.

## Summary

- FIP 50 Tickets and FIP Coupons are accepted by Vy, SJ Nord and Go-Ahead Nordic.
- Some cross-border connections and certain routes are excluded from FIP Discounts.
- Reservations are compulsory on certain routes.

## Class Categories

The classes on the trains differ depending on the operator and train category.

**Vy:**

- **Standard** (Lavpris / Flex): Comparable to 2nd class.
- **Pluss** (Plus): Comparable to 1st class. Complimentary coffee and tea are offered.
- **Kupé** (Compartment): Six-seat compartment. Only the entire compartment can be booked, not individual seats. Only available on day trains.
- **PlussNatt** (PlusNight): Reclining seat that can be laid completely flat. Only available on night trains.
- **Hvilekupé** (Rest): Couchette compartment with 6 berths. Only the entire compartment can be booked, not individual berths. Only available on night trains.
- **Sovekupé** (Sleeper): Sleeping compartment with 2 beds. Only available on night trains.

[More information about the classes at Vy.](https://www.vy.no/en/buy-tickets/train-tickets)

**SJ Nord:**

- **Standard**: Comparable to 2nd class.
- **Premium**: Comparable to 1st class. Complimentary cold and hot drinks are offered. Not available on night trains.
- **Premium Pluss** (Premium Plus): Reclining seat, complimentary cold and hot drinks, fruit and chocolate.
- **Sovekupé** (Sleeper): Sleeping compartment with two beds and a washbasin, with toilets in the corridor.

[More information about the classes at SJ Nord.](https://www.sj.no/en/ticket-categories/)

**Go-Ahead Nordic:**

- **Standard**: Comparable to 2nd class.
- **Ekstra** (Extra): Comparable to 1st class. Complimentary coffee, tea and snacks are offered.
- **Hvile** (Reclining Seat): Reclining seat that can be tilted by 45°. Otherwise like Ekstra. Available on night trains and selected day trains.
- **Sove** (Sleeper): Sleeping compartment with 2 beds. Only available on night trains.

[More information about the classes at Go-Ahead Nordic](https://go-aheadnordic.no/velkommen-om-bord/om-vare-reiseklasser)

Trains in categories `RE`, `R` and `L` usually have only Standard class.

With a 2nd class FIP Card / FIP Coupon, journeys in 1st class can be booked for a supplement.

FIP Tickets and reservations cannot be booked as self-service, but can be obtained via chat, telephone hotline or at the station ticket offices.

For reservations in seat, couchette or sleeper cars, a reservation fee must be paid regardless of the carriage class.

## Discounts

On public fares, children up to 6 years travel free of charge, children up to 18 years receive a discount on the adult fare. From the age of 18, the regular adult fare applies.[^2]

## Special Tariff Conditions

### Break of journey

A break of journey is not permitted.[^2]

### Cross-border journeys

FIP Discounts cannot be used on the following cross-border journeys[^1]:

- Halden – Gothenburg (RE20 is operated on this section by Vy Tåg Sverige)
- Oslo S – Kongsvinger – Charlottenberg – Stockholm (operated by SJ Sverige, therefore not valid on the entire route)
- Oslo S – Karlstad – Stockholm (operated by SJ Sverige, therefore not valid on the entire route)
- Narvik – Luleå (operated by SJ Sverige)

Only on the journey from Trondheim to Storlien is FIP recognized on the entire route.[^3]

### Journeys in the fare areas of Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim and Stavanger

**FIP Coupons** are valid in the train categories Regiontog and Lokaltog in the fare area of Oslo.[^2]

**FIP 50** is not valid in Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim or Stavanger if the journey begins and ends within the fare areas.[^1]

**More information about the fare areas:**

- Ruter (Oslo): https://ruter.no/en/about-our-tickets/zones-and-zone-maps
- Skyss (Bergen): https://www.skyss.no/en/tickets-and-prices/prize-zones
- AtB (Trøndelag): https://www.atb.no/en/zones-and-zone-maps
- Kolumbus (Rogaland): https://www.kolumbus.no/en/tickets/zones-and-zone-maps

{{% highlight tip %}}
It is possible to buy FIP 50 Tickets for journeys that partly run within these fare areas, as long as the journey does not take place entirely within one of these fare areas.[^1]
{{% /highlight %}}

### Oslo Airport

- FIP Discounts can be used on the route between Oslo Airport, Oslo and Drammen only on trains of Vy, SJ Nord and Go-Ahead Nordic (lines R10, R11, R12). More information can be found on the [Vy website about airport transfers](https://www.vy.no/en/travelling-with-vy/to-and-from-airport).
- Officially, the journey may not end in the fare zone of Oslo (Ruter), see [Journeys in the fare areas of Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim and Stavanger](#journeys-in-the-fare-areas-of-oslo-bergen-trondheim-and-stavanger).
- FIP cannot be used on Flytoget Airport Express trains.
- The long-distance trains from Trondheim to Oslo Airport (line F6) stop at the airport only for passengers alighting, which is why FIP is excluded here.

### Flåm Railway

The Flåm Railway between Myrdal and Flåm is operated by Vy, but is excluded from FIP Discounts.[^1]

### Ofoten Line

The Ofoten Line between Narvik and Bjørnfjell is excluded from FIP Discounts.[^1]
