The Societatea Natională de Transport Feroviar de Călători "CFR Călători" S.A., or CFR Călători for short, is the state railway company of [Romania](/country/romania "Romania") and operates the majority of passenger services on the Romanian rail network.

## Summary

- FIP 50 and FIP Coupons are accepted
- Reservation required on most long-distance trains and partially on regional services
- Limited booking options

## Validity of FIP Tickets

FIP Coupon: ✅ \
FIP Coupon for relatives: ⛔ \
FIP 50 Tickets: ✅

FIP Coupons and FIP 50 Tickets are valid on CFR Călători services. For cross-border journeys, either a continuous FIP 50 Ticket or FIP Coupons for both countries are required.

### On the Train

FIP-discounted tickets cannot be purchased on the train. Reservations must also be obtained before departure.

## Discounts

For regular tickets, children under 5 travel free of charge. Children under 10 receive a 50% discount on the adult fare. Older children pay the full adult fare.[^1]

## Special Tariff Conditions

### Break of journey

With FIP 50 tickets, a journey interruption is permitted only once and for a maximum of 24 hours from the time of arrival at the station where the journey is interrupted. To qualify, the ticket must be validated within one hour of arrival.[^1]

## Recommendations

{{% highlight tip %}}
CFR Călători provides a solid basic service on Romania's largely ageing rail network, especially on the main routes. Apart from the reservation requirement on most connections, travelling the country with FIP is relatively straightforward. Particular highlights include the international trains towards Western Europe as well as the well-known trains towards Moldova and Turkey, even though the latter cannot be used with FIP for the entire journey.
