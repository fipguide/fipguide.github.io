## Reservations

Booking requests for international trains between Serbia and Montenegro can be made by [telephone](tel:+381113602899) up to 30 days in advance. The reservations must be collected at the station in Serbia (Belgrade, Subotica) within 24 hours. The reservation centre can be reached from 9:00 to 17:00.[^1]
