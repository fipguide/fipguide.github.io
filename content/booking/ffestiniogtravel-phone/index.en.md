Ffestiniog Travel has direct access to the booking systems of many railway operators and can offer tickets for many trains in Europe.
On request, staff create a suitable offer for the journey.

We are currently aware that FIP Global Fare tickets can be issued for Eurostar.

## FIP Global Fare

FIP Global Fare tickets for Eurostar can be purchased via the Ffestiniog Travel hotline:

[+44 (0)1766 515630](tel:+441766515630)

Booking is available in English. A spelling alphabet can help when providing names and addresses. A booking fee of £ 5.00 applies.
