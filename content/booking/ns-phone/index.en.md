NS offers a booking hotline for tickets and reservations:

[+31 30 23 000 23](tel:+31302300023)

A credit card is required for booking. Payment is made via a link sent by email.

The hotline is available daily from 7 a.m. to 11 p.m.

A fee of € 7.50 per person (maximum € 22.50 per booking) applies to bookings. This fee does not apply to NS employees. It is unknown whether employees of other railway companies are also exempt from the fee.
