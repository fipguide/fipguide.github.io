At the following five railway stations in Norway, there is a ticket office where FIP 50 Tickets and reservations can be purchased:

- Bergen
- Oslo Airport (Gardermoen Station)
- Oslo Central (Oslo S)
- Stavanger
- Trondheim Central (Trondheim S)
