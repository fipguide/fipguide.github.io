## FIP 50 Tickets

FIP 50 Tickets for travel within Switzerland and to many European countries can be purchased at SBB ticket offices.

{{% /booking-section %}}

{{% booking-section "reservations" %}}

## Reservations

Reservations can be made for Switzerland as well as for most trains throughout Europe.

{{% /booking-section %}}

To avoid waiting times, a personal consultation appointment can be booked online in advance: [Book appointment](https://www.sbb.ch/de/hilfe-und-kontakt/produkte-services/weitere-services/weitere-sbb-services/beratung-reisezentrum.html)
