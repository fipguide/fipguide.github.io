## Reservations

Free reservations are available via GWR WhatsApp customer service.

To book, send a message to the WhatsApp number [+44 7890608043](https://wa.me/447890608043) with your travel details (date, time, departure and destination station) as well as the number of passengers. It is recommended to send the message in English.
