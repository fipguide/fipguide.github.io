## Reservations

The reservation price is calculated per journey. For connections with transfers, you only pay the price once for multiple trains.

Seat reservations for foreign and cross-border train connections can be booked via the DSB website. To book, select the option _Buy seat ticket only_ in the connection search.

Foreign reservations can be purchased for the following countries:

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
- Belgium
- Denmark
- Germany
- Luxembourg
- Netherlands
- Austria
- Czech Republic
