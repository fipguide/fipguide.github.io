## FIP 50 Tickets

At SŽ ticket offices, FIP 50 Tickets can be purchased for travel within Slovenia. At international ticket offices, they can also be purchased for trips to neighbouring countries.

A list of international ticket offices is available on the [SŽ website](https://potniski.sz.si/en/tickets/purchase-tickets-sz-and-ijpp/ticket-sales-outlets/).
{{% /booking-section %}}

{{% booking-section "reservations" %}}

## Reservations and Supplements

Seat reservations can be purchased on site for €1.50. If using an FIP Coupon, it must be presented.

Supplements for trains of categories ICS, IC, MV, EC and EN can also be purchased here. Current prices are available on the [SŽ website](https://potniski.sz.si/en/tickets/supplements-to-the-ticket-price/).

Different prices apply for couchettes and sleepers; ask on site.
