Koleje Małopolskie (KMŁ) has been the newest FIP member since 1 April 2026, further expanding the validity of FIP in Poland. This means that all voivodeship-owned railway companies can now be used with FIP discounts.

KMŁ provides regional train services in the Krakow area. With the trains, you can reach for example the well-known amusement park Energylandia (station Zator Park Rozrywki) or the Wieliczka Salt Mine (station Wieliczka-Rynek-Kopalnia). Oświęcim is also directly connected from Krakow via KMŁ.

Everything that is already known about FIP validity and booking options has been described on the [KMŁ page](/operator/kml). It can be expected that booking options will be gradually expanded with the additional channels known from other Polish railway companies (e.g. Koleo). The [Poland](/country/poland) page has also been updated and shows which operators are now all integrated into FIP.

Regarding FIP Coupons, please note that these may not yet be orderable from your respective railway company. A hint to the responsible department for travel discounts often helps, as the inclusion of KMŁ in FIP was announced at very short notice.
